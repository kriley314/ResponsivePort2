About Me

My name is Ken Riley and I'd like to welcome you to my page. I grew up here in Austin and attended The University of Texas from 1980-1984. I graduated in 1984 with a BS in Computer Engineering.

I worked doing software development for most of the next 30 years before taking a bit of time for myself to do some traveling and pursuing a few hobbies.

When I made the decision to pursue getting back into software development I found that my skill set had fallen behind to the point where I felt that my best option to be able to competitively contribute was to return to school and here I am!!
